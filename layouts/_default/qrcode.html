Simple idea

You give the destination. I make the QR code.

A QR code can open a menu, website, WhatsApp chat, Wi-Fi login, email, phone number, social media profile, plain text, or contact card. You send the exact destination and your ready logo. I turn it into a polished QR design and delivery files.
